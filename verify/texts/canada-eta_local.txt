What is the Canada eTA?

An electronic authorization linked to your passport that allows you to fly to Canada or connect at a Canadian airport without a physical visa.

5-Year Validity

With a single eTA you can enter and exit Canada as many times as you want for 5 years, or until your passport expires.

Approval in Minutes

Most applications receive automatic approval almost immediately. You will receive your eTA directly by email.

Linked to Your Passport

No physical stamp or sticker. The eTA is electronically linked to your biometric passport automatically.
