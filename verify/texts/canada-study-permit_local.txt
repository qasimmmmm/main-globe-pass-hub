COMMON MISTAKES
Don't Risk Rejection

Study permits are often denied for preventable reasons. Our expert review ensures your application is flawless.

Insufficient Proof of Funds

Many applicants fail to clearly demonstrate they can afford their studies and living expenses.

We meticulously review financial documents to ensure they meet IRCC standards.

Weak Study Plan

Failing to logically connect the chosen program in Canada to previous education or career goals.

We help you craft a compelling narrative that proves genuine intent to study.

Missing Documentation

Overlooking required forms or submitting incomplete documentation is an instant rejection trigger.

Our comprehensive checklist and final review guarantee nothing is missed.
