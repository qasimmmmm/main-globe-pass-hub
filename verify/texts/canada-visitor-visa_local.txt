COMMON MISTAKES
Why Visitor Visas Get Refused

Most TRV refusals are preventable. Our expert review ensures your application addresses every concern before submission.

Weak Ties to Home Country

Officers must believe you will return home. Insufficient evidence of employment, property, or family ties is the #1 reason for TRV refusal.

We identify and document your strongest home-country ties in a way visa officers find compelling.

Insufficient Proof of Funds

You must prove you can cover all costs without needing to work in Canada. Vague or incomplete bank statements are frequently rejected.

We guide you on presenting your finances correctly so they meet IRCC standards.

Unclear Purpose of Travel

Vague or unconvincing explanations for your visit raise red flags for immigration officers reviewing your file.

We help you craft a clear, credible cover letter that explains your visit and eliminates officer concerns.
