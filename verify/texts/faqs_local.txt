What services do you offer?
We provide assistance with the application process for electronic travel authorizations (eTAs), such as eTA for Canada. Our service includes reviewing your information, ensuring accuracy, submitting your application on your behalf to the official government platforms, and keeping you informed throughout the process.

OpenGov Services LLC is an independent service provider that facilitates the preparation and submission of administrative travel documentation. Our platform assists individuals and legal entities in completing the required steps for short-term entry procedures, following the guidelines established by the relevant Canadian authorities. Services are supervised by a Class L2 - RCIC certified immigration and citizenship consultant, licensed by the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC). You can verify our standing on the Official CICC Public Register.
Our Standard Processing fee is 99 USD, which includes the 7 CAD processing charge collected by Canadian immigration authorities. Please be advised that applicants may alternatively complete their Canada eTA request directly through the official Government of Canada website.
