Canada eTA — Simple & Secure

The Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) is a mandatory entry requirement for all visa-exempt travelers flying to or connecting through Canada. It is electronically linked to your biometric passport and is valid for up to 5 years or until your passport expires.

  • Mandatory for all visa-exempt nationals flying to Canada
  • Covers tourism, business, transit, and short-term visits
  • Linked to your passport — no physical document needed
  • Valid for multiple trips over 5 years
  • Processed and delivered directly to your inbox
